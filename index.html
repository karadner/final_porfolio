About Me

Hi and welcome to my travel blog! I'm Katie Radner, a sophomore at the University of Michigan. I am from Huntington Woods, Michigan. Here I will share all about my travels. I've been many places overseas, around the country, and even in my home state. Many of the places that I have traveled to were with my best friends from summer camp. We participated in a program for teens during the summer of 2018. We went to Italy, Israel, and Slovenia on a five week trip. From here, my love for traveling sparked. Now, I continue my passion for traveling with my friends and family. My mom and I have a shared fascination with traveling and experiencing new cultures. We have taken many trips together that are depicted throughout the site. I feel so fortunate to have been able to have had these amazing experiences, and I hope to take more trips to continue learning about the incredible world we live in.
